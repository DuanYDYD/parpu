Useful Tips

  • 1. Describe your Contest in as much detail as you can comfortably reveal - it will increase the quality of proposals you receive and shorten the selection process.
  • 2. Upload as much relevant information (pictures, documents, specifications, links, etc) as possible to get a realistic response.
  • 3. Match the target candidates to your requirements – remember, you’re looking for the best you can afford, not the cheapest you can get.
